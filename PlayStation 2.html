the PlayStation 2 came out in March 4, 2000, the original cost was $300. It came with the console, the cables to plug the console in, and a controller. The PlayStation 2 was the bestselling console to be recorded it sold 157 million units and sold 1.5 billion copies of games. The console has a power and reset button under that there is a eject button so the game disk comes out of the console so that you can play another game, on the left there are 2 memory card slots used for space there are 6 controller ports, and 2 USB’s. in the back of the console there is a power chord entrance, and another switch used to turn on the console. The controller has a d-pad, two joysticks, four buttons in the right triangle, circle, square, and x. in the middle of the controller there is a start button and a select button, under the start and select in the middle there is an analog button. In the back of the controller there are four buttons L, R, L2, and R2, which are used in game for stuff like shotting grabbing and more, in the joysticks you can also click the joysticks so that it can either select or do something in game. The ps2 has over 4,000 games and 1.5 billion game copies sold.
