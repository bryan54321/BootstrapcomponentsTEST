the first commercial home vidoe game console

you can insert different games and play but it would be black and white until they added color to the game

The Magnavox Odyssey came out in 1972, the original cost of the Odyssey was 99 dollars. The Magnavox Odyssey is used by attaching the odyssey antenna game switch to the terminals on the back of the tv. There is even a switch where you can change it to the normal tv or you can have it on the game. The Magnavox odyssey has game cards which you insert into the console after that you put the games paper on top of the tv screen and start playing. The controllers must be plugged in using the cable that is in the controller and you connect it onto the main console. The controllers has a nob on the sides of the controller you use those to move where the block goes one of them makes your block go to the left and the other one makes it go to the right. On the top of the controller there is a reset button which can be used to make the block or ball go back into the tv screen, there is also a nob on the back of the console which you can use to increase the speed of the ball, the English nob (smaller nob) is used to add spin to the ball which can be used in the game tennis.
